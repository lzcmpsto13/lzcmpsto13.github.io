Vault Ledger

Objective: As a collegiate vaulter, I ritually wrote down everything regarding my vault during a practice or competition. This includes the pole I was jumping with, where I ran from on the runway, what height or drill I was attempting, and more. Vault Ledger is a ledger for pole vaulters to maintain all these details in a easy and usable way. The website promoting this product was designed with a minimalist style in mind - fully responsive and clean.

Branding

About the Brand: The symbol to the right of "Vault Ledger" represents pole vault. It captures the moment right before a pole vaulter plants their pole in the box and catapults up, off the ground. The color scheme was chosen for its athletic and flat appeal.

The Product

The Ledger: Each section of the Vault Ledger has a purpose. The website helps show how each section should be used.

Coffee Time-Line

Objective: Coffee Time-Line is a content-focused website design. The structure of the homepage resembles a time line where the user can scroll through the history of coffee. Interior pages provide additional information about each period in time. A custom icon set was designed for one interior page to illustrate the differences between various espresso drinks.

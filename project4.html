Iterations

Objective: Create a website design for a faux e-magazine company. Content provided by articles written by peers.

The Design: The homepage is a grid of images with a corresponding article title and a blurb from the article. Once clicked, users are taken to a page where they can read the article in its entirety. The navigation of the website design in intended to be responsive. On mobile devices, the navigation will display as a slender bar on the left-hand side of the screen with appropriate, custom-designed icons. For laptop and larger screen, the navigation will extend to the right, showing a corresponding link name.



Navigation

Responsive Navigation: The navigation of the site is designed to be interactive and responsive. On hover, users will achieve the second state of the navigation and then, once a link is clicked, the sub nav will appear as shown in the animation.

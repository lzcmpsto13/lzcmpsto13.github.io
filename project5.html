Be Central

Objective: To design a t-shirt for the end of the year senior bar crawl in order celebrate the graduation of the class of 2015.

The Design: Overall, I wanted to create a retro style using appropriate colors and layout. The bird illustration is an interpretation of the North Central mascot Chippy the Cardinal. The North Central College slogan "Be Central" was altered to say "Beer Central" in celebration of the bar crawl.
